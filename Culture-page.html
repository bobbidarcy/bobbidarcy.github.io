Denver Art Museum
Denver offers many activities that allow you to experience different cultures. The Denver Art Museum is one of those locations.
Open daily from 10am to 5pm, until 9pm on Tuesdays, this museum offers a wide range of art exhibits and collections that are exciting for all ages.

One of their current exhibits includes Modern Woman/ Modern Vision. This exhibit features work by over 100 women artists.

Denver Museum of Nature and Science
This museum has been open to the public since 1908. With a focus on science, people of all ages come to explore the museum's amazing exhibits!
The museum is open seven days a week year-round, excluding Christmas Day.
